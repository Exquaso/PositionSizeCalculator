Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 29, 2024.

1. Introduction

Welcome to the Stock Position Size Calculator app. We are committed to protecting your privacy and want to inform you about how we handle data within the app.

2. Data Collection

Our app does not collect any personal information from users. However, the app syncs calculation inputs and settings with iCloud to ensure your data is backed up and accessible across your Apple devices.

3. Use of Data

The data synced with iCloud is used solely for backup and synchronization purposes. This data is not accessed, used, or shared by us.

4. Data Security

We rely on Apple's iCloud services to securely store your data. For more information on how iCloud data is protected, please refer to Apple's privacy policy.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app and will take effect immediately upon posting. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at contact@exquaso.com.
